MOOT Court Competition

A tool for organizing and tracking competition rounds.

Created April 23, 2025

How it works:

This app creates random pairings for teams in your competition. Teams are matched for preliminary rounds, with winners proceeding to elimination rounds. The system ensures fair matchups by switching petitioner/respondent roles and preventing teams from facing each other twice.
